Our Lab

We are a undergraduate student-led research lab at Morgan State University working on exciting projects in artificial intelligence, human-robot interaction, and quantum computing. The lab operates under the guidance of Dr. Paul Wang, Chair and Professor of the Department of Computer Science.

Research Projects

NAO Robot Assistant

By: Aayush Shrestha

This project integrates OpenAI Whisper (speech-to-text), GPT for conversational logic, and NAOqi motion control to turn the NAO humanoid robot into a smart assistant that responds to voice commands and engages in meaningful dialogue.

View GitHub Repository →

MSU CS Navigator

By: Sakina Shrestha

This RAG chatbot is designed to assist students with common questions related to the Morgan State CS Department. Built with FastAPI, Pinecone, and OpenAI, it’s deployed on AWS and integrates into the NAO robot for voice support.

View GitHub Repository →

Quantum Entanglement & Qiskit

By: Julian Gordon

This project uses the quED and quCR experimental setup to explore quantum entanglement, measure polarization correlations of photon pairs, and analyze violations of Bell’s inequality to demonstrate quantum nonlocality.
