Project Summary

Under the supervision of Dr. Paul Wang, Chair of the Computer Science Department at Morgan State University, this project integrates OpenAIâ€™s GPT and Whisper models with the NAO6 humanoid robot to enhance its conversational and interactive capabilities. The system enables NAO to understand spoken language, generate intelligent responses, and interact naturally with users.

Key Achievements

  • Developed a Flask-based backend server for handling speech transcription, OpenAI responses, and database logging.
  • Implemented face recognition for personalized greetings and user identification.
  • Designed multi-mode personalities (e.g., study mode, general mode) with adjustable tone and pitch.
  • Currently expanding to Pinecone vector search for memory-based conversation and knowledge retrieval.
